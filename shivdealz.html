Our Story

Fashion for Every Chapter of Life

ShivDealz was founded in 2022 with a simple mission: to make people feel amazing without breaking the bank. We curate the finest fashion pieces and bring them to you at prices that genuinely make you smile.

From our humble beginnings as a WhatsApp-based boutique in Mumbai, we've grown into a full-fledged fashion brand trusted by over 12,000 happy customers across India. Every stitch, every cut, every collection is handpicked by our team of passionate stylists.

We believe fashion is a form of self-expression, and everyone deserves to express themselves beautifully — every single day.
